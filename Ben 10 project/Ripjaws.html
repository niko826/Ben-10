1.General Info:
Name: Ripjaws
Species: Piscciss Volann
Home Planet: Piscciss
First Appearance: The Krakken episode

2.Appearance
Has a humanoid fish-look: parts of alligators, eels, angler fish, sharks.
Can switch between legs (on land) and a fish tail (in water).
Greenish/gray skin, phosphorescent light (lure) on his head for seeing in dark water.


3.Abilities & Powers
1.Sharp teeth & large jaws that can shred metal.
2.Sharp claws for cutting/slashing.
3.Can breathe underwater and swim at very high speed with agility.
4.Strong enough to lift more than his own weight; durable enough to punch through solid objects.
5.Tail/legs transformation, allows flexibility between land and water.
6.Glowing lure helps see in dark or deep water.
7.Endurance against water pressure, including very deep or icy environments.
8.Lower jaw can unhinge to hold or swallow large objects.

4.Weaknesses
1.Can’t stay out of water too long (will dehydrate.)
2.Exposure to heat accelerates dehydration.
3.Vulnerable to electricity and very strong currents.
