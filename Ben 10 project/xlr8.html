1.General Info:
Name: XLR8
Species: Kineceleran
Home Planet: Kinet (stormy, high-gravity world)
First Appearance: Ben 10 (original 2005 series)

2.Appearance
1.Resembles a semi-armored velociraptor.
2.Black body with blue stripes and a green Omnitrix symbol on his chest.
3.Helmet with a visor featuring an "X" design.
4.Wheels on his feet and a tail with blue stripes.
5.Three claws on each hand and a prehensile tail.


3.Abilities & Powers
1.Super Speed: Can run at speeds up to 800 km/h (497 mph).
2.Enhanced Reflexes: Quick reaction times and agility.
3.Friction Manipulation: Can reduce friction to move faster.
4.Vortex Creation: Able to create localized tornadoes by running in circles.
5.Enhanced Recovery: Recovers quickly from negative effects like stunning or fatigue.
6.Accelerated Thinking: Processes information rapidly, perceiving time as slowed down.

4.Weaknesses
1.Magnetic Fields & Charged Pulses: Can disrupt his movement and cause loss of control.
2.Surface Limitations: Struggles on icy or sticky surfaces, affecting his speed and stability.
3.Tail Immobilization: If his tail is held down, it can hinder his movement.
4.Opponent Reflexes: Enemies with quick reflexes can counter his speed and land a hit.
