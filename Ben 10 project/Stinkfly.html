1.General Info:
Insectoid body with wings; four legs, two arms.
Greenish wings, fragile and patterned.
Has multiple eye stalks (eyes on stalks) giving wide vision.
Sharp tail, claws on limbs.

2.Appearance
Insectoid body with wings; four legs, two arms.
Greenish wings, fragile and patterned.
Has multiple eye stalks (eyes on stalks) giving wide vision.
Sharp tail, claws on limbs.


3.Abilities & Powers
1. can fly fast, change directions quickly in midair.
2.360° Vision: eye stalks let him see around, even behind.
3.Slime / Goo Projection: shoots sticky or toxic slime from wings, mouth, or eye stalks.
4.Poison / Toxic Gas & Saliva: can emit gases or toxins that harm or disable.
5.Sharp Tail & Claws: tail can slice; limbs useful for slashing/catching.
6.Enhanced Strength (relative to insect body): can carry heavy loads while flying.
7.Durable Exoskeleton: tough shell protects body, though not invincible.

4.Weaknesses
1.Wings are fragile: damage or water on wings reduces or stops flight.
2.Sensitive to chemical poisons or harsh gases.
3.Cannot swim well: limbs not built for aquatic movement.
4.Odor: strong body smell makes stealth/hiding harder.
5.His own toxin/gases can backfire in enclosed spaces.
