1.General Info:
Name: Diamondhead
Species: Petrosapien
Home Planet: Petropia (rocky, crystalline world)
First Appearance: Ben 10 (original 2005 series)

2.Appearance
Made almost entirely of green crystal.
Humanoid shape with sharp edges and facets.
Crystals cover his body like armor, giving him a shiny, diamond-like look.
Stands taller than an average human.

3.Abilities & Powers
1.Crystal Generation & Manipulation Can create crystal weapons (blades, shields, spikes). Can shape crystals into walls or barriers for defense.
2.Super Strength Stronger than most human-sized aliens. Can punch, lift, and throw heavy objects with ease.
3.Durability / Invulnerability His crystal body makes him highly resistant to physical attacks. Can survive bullets, explosions, and other impacts.
4.Energy Reflection Can reflect laser beams or energy attacks off his crystal surfaces.
5.Regeneration Crystals can regrow if broken, although it takes some effort.

4.Weaknesses
1.Sonic Vibrations: His crystalline structure is highly susceptible to sonic vibrations, which can shatter his body.
2.Regeneration Limits: If completely disassembled, he cannot regenerate himself.
3.Back Shards: The shards on his back can cause him to become immobilized if knocked into walls or other surfaces.
4.Crystal Entrapment: Diamondhead can be trapped within crystal formations.
5.Cold Virus Susceptibility: He can be affected by the cold virus, which can impact his crystalline structure and abilities.
