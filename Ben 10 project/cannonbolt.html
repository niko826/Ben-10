1.General Info:
Name: Cannonbolt
Species: Arburian Pelarota
Home Planet: Arburia (destroyed)

2.Appearance
Broad, muscular build with yellow armor plating covering back, shoulders, and the backs of arms
Mostly white body with black parts on upper half
Four sharp claws on each paw, small sharp teeth
Has yellow eyes


3.Abilities & Powers
1.Can curl into a tough armored ball (sphere form) for protection
2.When rolled up, moves at high speed; can slam into or run over enemies
3.In sphere form can ricochet / bounce off surfaces, sustain momentum, even use airborne maneuvers
4.Shell is very durable: resists acids, lava, extreme heat (e.g. reentry heat), and can deflect lasers or energy attacks
5.Enhanced strength, durability, reflexes, agility, jumping, balance
6.Good hearing; can sense motion and surroundings even when eyes are obstructed (via vibration, “fur whisker”-type sensing)
7.Can carry or shelter people/objects inside his shell while curled
8.can survive severe environments (e.g. vacuum of space) for short periods (though still needs to breathe)

4.Weaknesses
1.Hard to stop or control direction once rolling at high speed (due to momentum)
2.His upright form (when not curled) is top-heavy, so he’s clumsy and unbalanced on land in that form
3.Struggles on slippery or unstable surfaces; can’t roll properly on sand, slime, etc.
4.Can be trapped or held in place by very strong beings or adhesives
5.Vulnerable to electricity when not protected by his shell
6.Limited mobility in tight spaces or confined areas because of bulk.
