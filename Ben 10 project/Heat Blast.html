1.General Info:
Name:Heatblast
Species: Pyronite
Home Planet: Pyros (volcanic world)
Ben 10 (original 2005 series)

2.Appearance
Humanoid figure composed of molten magma encased in dark red volcanic rocks.
Radiates intense heat, with glowing yellow cracks across his body.
Flame-like mane atop his head and fiery tongue.
Stands approximately 7 feet tall.


3.Abilities & Powers
1.Pyrokinesis – Generate and control fire.
2.Fire Constructs – Create weapons and barriers from flames.
3.Flight – Propel himself using fire.
4.Enhanced Strength & Durability – Superhuman physical capabilities.
5.Fire Immunity – Resistant to heat and fire.
6.Underwater Breathing – Can breathe underwater despite being fire-based.
7.Light & Smoke Generation – Emit light and create smoke.
8.Fire Breath & Spit – Exhale and project fire.
9.Cryokinesis (via Cold Virus) – Ability to manipulate ice when infected.

4.Weaknesses
1.Water Exposure – Water can extinguish his flames, rendering him powerless.
2.Wind Gusts – Strong winds can disperse his fire.
3.Fire Extinguishers – Substances designed to put out fires can neutralize him.
4.Overheating – Excessive use of his powers can lead to exhaustion.
